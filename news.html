Explanation of Color Mixing in Printed Patterns

Why minor color mixing can appear where colors overlap, contrast is strong or printed details are especially small.

During spray printing on kraft paper, minor color mixing can occur where several colors overlap, where adjacent areas have strong contrast, or where artwork contains very small details and gradients. Understanding those conditions early helps teams adapt artwork and expectations before production.
