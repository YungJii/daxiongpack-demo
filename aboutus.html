Chaozhou Chaoan Daxiong Color Printing Co., Ltd.

A modern printing company shaped by more than three decades of production.

Founded in 1993, Daxiong specializes in the wholesale, research, production and sales of flexible packaging. Our work covers coffee bags, bakery bags, pet food bags, rice bags, shaped pouches, packaging films and other food packaging formats.

Our goal is straightforward: work closely with partners and help customers achieve greater success across different product categories. That means combining manufacturing capability with design support, responsible communication and practical customization.
