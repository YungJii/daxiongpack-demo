Common questions

Useful answers before the first email.

01What information should I prepare for a quote?

Bag type, finished size, material or barrier requirement, estimated quantity, printing artwork and destination are the most useful starting points.

02Can Daxiong Pack help with packaging design?

Yes. The original support information describes a professional design and service team that can assist with packaging design and production preparation.

03Which printing methods are available?

The support page lists gravure, flexographic, digital and jet printing options. The suitable method depends on format, quantity, artwork and delivery requirements.

04Are stock packaging products available?

Yes. General coffee bags, bakery bags and stand-up pouch formats are available in stock specifications for faster sourcing.
